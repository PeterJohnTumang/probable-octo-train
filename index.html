Summer Holiday

Estrella Falls

Estrella Falls is a Twin Waterfall located within a forest. It is known for its fauna and flora, offering cool water pools and shaded areas that good for picnics and swimming activity. You can feel the coolness and freshness of flowing water coming from the falls.

Hidden Beach

Hidden beach are surrounded by limestone rocks formations. The swimming area resembles a natural infinity pool with crystal clear waters. The serenity of the place is manifested with the whistling of birds. Survivor Sweden finished their three month filming at this beach. This beach still has the some of the props used at the show.

Long Beach

San Vicente's Long Beach is the longest white-sand beach in the Philippines and the second-longest in Southeast Asia. The 14.7km stretch of powdery sand is lined with endless towering coconut trees and a few nipa huts.

Sugba Lagoon

Sugba Lagoon is well-known for its emerald waters surrounded by the equally beautiful Del Carmen Mangrove Reserve. Here, you can do a variety of aquatic activities or try cliff jumping. For beach lovers, Kawhagan and Pamomoan Islands are another reason to fall in love with Siargao.

The Ruins

The Ruins Mansion is the remains of an ancestral home built in 1920 by a wealthy sugar baron named Don Mariano Ledesma Lacson. Don Mariano constructed the mansion as a testament of his love for his Portuguese wife, Cora Maria Osorio Rosa-Braga, after she passed away in 1911. Maria, who was pregnant with their eleventh child at the time, lost her and their baby’s lives after figuring in an accident.

Vigan

Vigan city is located in the northern part of the Philippines and it’s historical center, which is called the heritage village, is listed on the UNESCO world heritage list since 2 December 1999 for it’s unique spanish colonial history in Asia and the very well preserved spanish monumental buildings which where build in the 18th century.
